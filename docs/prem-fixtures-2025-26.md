# Gallagher Premiership 2025/26 — Fixtures

Reference fixture list for the 2025/26 Gallagher Premiership Rugby season. Used to seed a season schedule in the simulator if/when a league mode is added.

## Season structure

- **10 clubs:** Bath, Bristol Bears, Exeter Chiefs, Gloucester, Harlequins, Leicester Tigers, Newcastle Red Bulls (rebranded from Falcons), Northampton Saints, Sale Sharks, Saracens.
- **18 regular-season rounds** (each club plays the other nine home and away).
- **Top 4** progress to playoffs: 1 v 4 and 2 v 3 semi-finals hosted by the higher seed, then a one-off final at Twickenham.
- Broadcast: TNT Sports / Discovery+ (HBO Max in the US) for all 93 matches; selected matches free-to-air on ITV including the final.
- Breaks align with the Autumn Nations Series (Nov) and the Six Nations (Feb–mid-Mar).
