# European Cups 2025-26

Research document covering the Investec Champions Cup and EPCR Challenge Cup 2025/26 season — format, pools, full fixture list, and knockout results. Used as the reference for implementing European competition in the game.

---

## 1. Overview

Both competitions are run by **European Professional Club Rugby (EPCR)** under the Investec / EPCR sponsorship umbrella. The 2025/26 season is the twelfth edition of the Champions Cup.

**EPCR Finals Weekend:** San Mamés Stadium, Bilbao — Friday 22 May (Challenge Cup final) + Saturday 23 May 2026 (Champions Cup final).

---

## 2. Investec Champions Cup 2025-26

### 2.1 Format

- **24 clubs** — 8 each from the English Premiership, French Top 14, and URC (including South African and Italian sides).
- **4 pools of 6**, with 2 clubs from each league per pool. Clubs from the same URC Shield cannot share a pool.
- **Pool stage:** each club plays **4 matches** — one against each cross-league opponent only (no intra-league pool matches).
- **Top 4 from each pool** advance to the Round of 16 (16 teams total, single-leg knockout).
- **5th-placed teams** from each pool drop into the Challenge Cup Round of 16.
- **6th-placed teams** are eliminated.

## 3. EPCR Challenge Cup 2025-26

### 3.1 Format

- **18 clubs** enter at the pool stage — from Premiership, Top 14, and URC. Plus 2 invited clubs (Black Lion of Georgia and Toyota Cheetahs of South Africa).
- **3 pools of 6**, with no more than 2 teams from the same nation in a pool (France has 2 per pool but they don't face each other in the pool stage).
- **Pool stage:** 4 rounds — same cross-league match structure as Champions Cup.
- **Top 4 from each pool** (12 teams) advance to the Round of 16.
- **4 additional teams** enter the Round of 16 from the Champions Cup — the 5th-placed side from each Champions Cup pool.
- **Total R16:** 16 clubs, single-leg knockout.
